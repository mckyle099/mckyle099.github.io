Parameters:

buildMarket
Signature: buildMarket(position)
Parameters:
   position: A value between 1 and 9 that specifies the position to build the building
Return: True on success, false otherwise
Description: Builds a Market at specified position. Having a Market unlocks functions that let you buy and sell resources.
Market:
   Health: 10000
   Functionality: buyPopulation
   Functionality: sellPopulation
   Functionality: buyWood
   Functionality: sellWood
Cost:
   Population: 5
   Wood: 400


repairBuilding
Signature: repairBuilding(position, amount)
Parameters:
   position: A value between 1 and 9 that specifies the position of the building to repair
   amount: How much to HP to repair the building by. Each HP repaired costs 1 wood.
Return: True on success, false otherwise
Description: Repairs a building. Costs 1 wood per hp repaired.

buyPopulation
Signature: buyPopulation(amount)
Parameters:
   amount: An integer specifying how much population you want to buy
Return: True on success, False otherwise
Description: Trades gold for population. 1 population costs 11 gold.
Requirements: Player must have a Market


sellPopulation
Signature: sellPopulation(amount)
Parameters:
   amount: An integer specifying how much population you want to sell
Return: True on success, False otherwise
Description: Trades population for gold. 1 population sells for 9 gold.
Requirements: Player must have a Market


buyWood
Signature: buyWood(amount)
Parameters:
   amount: An integer specifying how much wood you want to buy. Must be a multiple of 10.
Return: True on success, False otherwise
Description: Trades gold for wood. 10 wood costs 11 gold.
Requirements: Player must have a Market


sellWood
Signature: sellWood(amount)
Parameters:
   amount: An integer specifying how much wood you want to sell. Must be a multiple of 10.
Return: True on success, False otherwise
Description: Trades wood for gold. 10 wood sells for 9 gold.
Requirements: Player must have a Market
